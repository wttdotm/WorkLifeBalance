Welcome to Work Life Balance

Work Life Balance is an extension made to encourage you to balance work and play.

Health Mode or POS Mode

Health mode tells you to take a break from work to go have fun, or take a break from slacking off to get some work done.

POS mode simply plays distracting fun alert sound when you are working, or a work alert sound when you are relaxing.

Type: 'POS' or 'Health'

Interval Range

How often would you like us to check in on you?

Health mode uses this as a regular interval.

POS mode uses this as an upper bound for a random interval.

Type a number in seconds. 60 = 1 minute. 3600 = 1 hour.
